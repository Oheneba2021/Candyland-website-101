Board of Directors

The TIMES² Incorporated Board of Directors is the governing body of TIMES² Academy. The Board of Directors is charged with making all top level decisions for the school. We are currently recruiting new members to the Board of Directors. If you are interested in learning more about opportunities to serve on the TIMES² Incorporated Board of Directors, please email Jasmin Blackmar at jblackmar@times2.org

Stay tuned for more information about our annual Board of Directors meeting!

Meeting Schedule

TIMES² Inc. board meetings begin at 5:00 pm and are held in the High School Cafeteria unless otherwise specified. Board meetings are open to the public. Dates and times are subject to change, but will be posted. An agenda will be posted as soon as it is approved to this page. If you are unable to attend, but would like to call in and participate via conference call, please contact Linda Mousseau at 401.272.5094, ext. 400 for the number to call and the password. * All dates are subject to change please check the day before post date on this website for updates.
