Athletics

Welcome to Athletics at CANDYLAND SCHOOL

Our Athletic Department's mission is to instill a sense of accomplishment and sportsmanship through a number of sports for both middle and high school. For high school, we are a part of the Rhode Island Interscholastic League.

 

High School Athletics

We offer both Fall and Spring sports. For schedules, visit our RIIL Page.

 

Middle School Athletics

Our sports

Boys/Girls Basketball

We offer both boys' and girls' basketball in both middle and high school. For our full schedules for high school, please see our RIIL page. 

Coaches: 

Boys Varsity - Chris Love

 

Boys/Girls Track and Field

We offer boys' and girls' track and field teams in the Spring. For full schedules, see our RIIL page.

Boys/Girls Soccer

Soccer is offered in the Fall. 

Coaches:

Boys Varsity - Kay Ekutti

Girls Varsity - Chris Love

Boys/Girls Volleyball

Girls' Volleyball is one of our Fall sports while Boys' Volleyball is offered in the Spring.

Coaches:

Girls Varsity - Jahmal Diggs, head coach - Eric Wilson, assistant coach

Boys Baseball/Girls Softball

Both boys' baseball and girls' softball are offered in the Spring

Coaches:
