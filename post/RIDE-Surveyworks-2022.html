RIDE Surveyworks 2022

February 28, 2022 | by bbennett

Every year, RIDE (Rhode Island Department of Education) has a series of surveys for the community, this includes school faculty and staff as well as students and families. Responses to these surveys help us see how we are doing and ways that we can improve.

The deadline to complete the RIDE Surveyworks for 2022 is March 18!

Families are encouraged to fill out the survey here: surveys.panoramaed.com/ride/ridefamily

Your voice is important to us and will help us as we continue to support our community and grow together!

Please take a few minutes and fill out the survey!
